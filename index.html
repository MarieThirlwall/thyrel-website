About Thyrel

Thyrel Ltd was founded by Marie Thirlwall as a microSME helping businesses break through into markets. The term þyrel in Old English refers to a hole or aperture — fitting perfectly with the challenges of opening new windows into customer insights, or piercing through existing markets to secure new advantages.

We support customers with business services along their entire journey, from discovery to maximising market potential.
