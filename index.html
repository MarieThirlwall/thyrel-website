About Thyrel

Thyrel Ltd was founded by Marie Thirlwall as a microSME helping businesses break through into markets. The term þyrel, or 'Thyrel' in Old English refers to a hole or aperture. This seemed very fitting with the challenges of opening new windows into customer insights, or piercing through existing markets to secure a growth or impact advantage.
